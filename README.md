# Olist E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project is an end-to-end Business Intelligence dashboard built using Power BI on the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset.

The dashboard analyzes sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, payment patterns, review scores, and logistics efficiency to generate actionable business insights.

---

## Business Objective

The main objective of this project is to help an e-commerce business understand:

* Revenue performance
* Customer behavior
* Product category performance
* Delivery efficiency
* Seller performance
* Customer satisfaction
* Operational bottlenecks

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview

This page provides a high-level view of business performance.

Key metrics:

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Total Customers
* Average Order Value
* Total Products Sold
* Revenue Growth
* Order Status Breakdown

---

### 2. Customer & Quality Analytics

This page focuses on customer behavior and satisfaction.

Key insights:

* Customer distribution
* Review score analysis
* Orders per customer
* Customer satisfaction trends
* Negative review patterns

---

### 3. Product & Logistics Analytics

This page analyzes product sales, delivery performance, and logistics efficiency.

Key insights:

* Top product categories
* Seller performance
* Freight cost analysis
* Average delivery days
* On-time vs late deliveries
* Logistics bottlenecks

## Key Business Insights

* Product categories show significant variation in revenue contribution.
* Delivery delays directly impact customer review scores.
* Certain sellers and product categories contribute more strongly to revenue.
* Freight cost plays an important role in logistics performance.
* Customer satisfaction can be improved by reducing late deliveries.
* Revenue growth trends help identify high-performing periods.
